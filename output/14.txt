At a Sunset Boulevard mansion, the body of Joe Gillis floats in the swimming pool. Ushered in by Max, the butler, Joe recognizes the woman as long-forgotten silent film star Norma Desmond. 
 Norma lavishes attention on Joe and buys him expensive clothes. When Joe phones Max to have him pack his things, Max tells him Norma cut her wrists with his razor. Joe returns to Norma.
 Norma has Max deliver the edited Salome script to her former director Cecil B. As Joe walks away from the house, Norma shoots him three times and he falls into the pool.
