On a bus for his first day of school, Forrest meets Jenny, with whom he immediately falls in love, and they become best friends. After graduating, Forrest enlists in the United States Army, where he befriends former shrimp fisherman Benjamin Buford Bubba Blue , and they agree to go into the shrimping business together once they end their service. Dan is scornful of Forrests plans to enter the shrimping business and mockingly promises to be Forrests first mate if he ever succeeds. During his reunion with Jenny, Forrest discovers they have a young son, also named Forrest . Forrest waits with Forrest Jr. for the bus to pick him up for his first day of school, and watches his feather bookmark float off in the wind.
