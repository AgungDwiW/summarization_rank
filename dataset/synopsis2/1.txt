Vitos youngest son, Michael, in a Marine Corp uniform, introduces his girlfriend, Kay Adams, to his family at the sprawling reception. Michael comes up with a plan to hit Sollozzo and Mc Cluskey: on the pretext of settling the dispute, Michael accepts their offer to meet in a Bronx restaurant and, retrieving a planted handgun, murders them. When Michael travels to Las Vegas to buy out Greenes stake in the familys casinos, Greene derides the Corleones as a fading power.Vito collapses and dies in his garden while playing with Michaels son, Anthony. After Carlo is questioned by Michael on his involvement in setting up Sonnys murder and confesses he was contacted by Barzini, Peter Clemenza kills him with a wire garrote.
